How to Create a Blurred Frame for Your Image

  1. Upload Your Image: Click on 'Upload Image' and select the image you wish to edit.
  2. Adjust Image Scale: Use the 'Main Image Scale' slider to resize your image, fitting it within the desired area on the canvas.
  3. Set Background Image Scale: Use the 'Background Image Scale' slider to adjust the scale of the background. This creates a larger version of your image in the background.
  4. Apply Blur to Background: Adjust the 'Blur Intensity' slider to apply a blur effect to the background image. This creates a blurred frame effect around your main image.
  5. Download Your Edited Image: Once you are satisfied with the blurred frame effect, click the 'Download Image' button to save your work.

This easy-to-follow process allows you to add a sophisticated blurred frame to your images, enhancing their aesthetic appeal with minimal effort.
