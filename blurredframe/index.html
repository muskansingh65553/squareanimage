Steps to Create a Blurred Frame for Your Image

  1. Upload Your Image: Click on 'Upload Image' and choose the picture you want to edit.
  2. Adjust Image Size: Use the 'Main Image Scale' slider to make your image smaller or larger so it fits nicely on the canvas.
  3. Set Background Image Size: Use the 'Background Image Scale' slider to make the background bigger or smaller. This makes a larger version of your image behind it.
  4. Apply Blur to Background: Change the 'Blur Intensity' slider to make the background blurry. This makes a nice blurry frame around your main image.
  5. Download Your Edited Image: When you're happy with the blurry frame, click the 'Download Image' button to save your work.

This simple process lets you easily add a stylish blurred frame to your images, making them look even better with just a few clicks.
